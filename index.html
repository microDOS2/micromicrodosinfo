Safety & Use Notes

  • Start Low: If you are new to Metocin, begin with a light microdose (1/4 tab) to assess your sensitivity.
  • Legal: 4‑HO‑MET is not federally scheduled in the U.S.; always verify your local laws.
  • Set & setting: While microdosing is sub-perceptual, be mindful of your environment. Avoid driving until you understand how it affects you.
  • Plan your day: Effects are subtle but can last ~4 hours.
  • Hydration: Drink water and avoid mixing with alcohol.
  • Adults only: Intended for 21+. Consult a healthcare professional if you have medical conditions or take other medications.
